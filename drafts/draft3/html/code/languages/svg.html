The Canvas

As in art, SVG graphics appear on a canvas.

Example

The perimeter of a rectangle is shown on this 300 by 100 canvas.


<svg
 width="300"
 height="100"
>
 <rect
  x="0"
  y="0"
  width="300"
  height="100"
  fill="transparent" 
  stroke="black" 
  stroke-width="1"
 />
</svg>

Example

This canvas has a width of 300 and a height of 100. The perimeter of a rectangle, this time with a width and height 2 less than those of the canvas, is drawn in black.


<svg
 width="300"
 height="100"
>
 <rect
  x="1"
  y="1"
  width="298"
  height="98"
  fill="transparent" 
  stroke="black" 
  stroke-width="1"
 />
</svg>
