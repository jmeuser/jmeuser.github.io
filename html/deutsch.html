How to Work in English and German on OS X 10.10.5 
1 Go to system preferences.
2 On the top row, select Language & Region.
3 Below the box labeled preferred languages select the + sign.
4 Choose the Deutsch you desire.
5 Click on keyboard preferences.
6 Select + in bottom left.
7 Add German keyboard.
